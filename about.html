Fabiana is a Venezuelan photographer and filmmaker based in Lisbon, and available worldwide, whose artistic journey began in Buenos Aires, Argentina.
Her studies in photography and art direction ignited her passion for visual storytelling, and she quickly established herself as a freelance photographer and films assistant. With experience at agencies like Abad Studios, she has made her way in the fashion industry, creating captivating work both, personal and commissioned for clients that varies between fashion, commercial and skin care photography and fashion films.
What sets Fabiana apart is her unique perspective as an immigrant and global citizen. Having relocated at a young age, she embraces the beauty of multiculturalism and diverse human roots. Her photography celebrates the intrinsic beauty of the human body, capturing the connection between humanity and the outward expression of our inner souls. Through her lens, she invites viewers to discover the remarkable beauty and diversity that exist in our world.
Driven by her personal experiences, Fabiana's work carries a powerful message of unity and appreciation for unfamiliar cultures. Her captivating images compel people worldwide to see the world through her eyes, uncovering the beauty in every corner. With her unwavering commitment to storytelling, Fabiana invites us on a visual journey that transcends borders and leaves us in awe of the remarkable people and places that inhabit our planet. Her work seeks to blur the boundaries between documentary and fashion.
